Arcade Room Project


This project was a group projects to build a scene of arcade room. Each member built their own arcade machine, and place them with props to make a well-designed arcade room. This project made our group to apply overall 3D skills with programs such as Autodesk Maya or Substance 3D Painter, and skills for shooting shots.
